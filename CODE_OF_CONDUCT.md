# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want this project to be a welcoming, constructive, and respectful space for scientists, students, developers, and users working with chemoinformatics and Orange workflows.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional in discussions, reviews, and issue reports.
- Focus feedback on the code, workflow, reproducibility, or user impact.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before jumping to conclusions.
- Share reproducible examples, logs, screenshots, or small datasets when reporting problems.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Sharing private credentials, personal data, or proprietary datasets without permission.
- Hostile or dismissive responses to beginners or students.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation contributions, and project-related communication.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer at `crtomir.podlipnik@fkkt.uni-lj.si`.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participation when needed to keep the project constructive and safe.
